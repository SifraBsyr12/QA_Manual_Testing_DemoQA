# Test Case: TC012 - Submit Form with Invalid Email

**Feature:** Practice Form  
**Test Case ID:** TC012  
**Priority:** Medium  

**Steps to Execute:**
1. Open Practice Form page
2. Enter valid First Name and Last Name
3. Enter invalid Email (e.g. test@)
4. Fill other mandatory fields
5. Click **Submit**

**Expected Result:**  
Form should not be submitted. Email field should show validation error.

**Actual Result:**  
Form should not be submitted. Email field show validation error.
